About Leaf-Note

Your trusted partner in Zambian real estate.

Our Story

Leaf-Note Financial Services & Real Estate Ltd was founded to demystify and simplify property ownership in Zambia. We are a premier real estate and financial services firm dedicated to helping individuals and businesses find their dream property with flexible, accessible financing options.

As a trusted partner, we are committed to navigating the complexities of the property market on behalf of our clients, ensuring every transaction is smooth, transparent, and beneficial. We are proudly affiliated with PACRA and operate with the highest standards of integrity.

Our Mission

To make property ownership in Zambia accessible and affordable through expert guidance, extensive property listings, and innovative financing solutions that empower our clients to build their future.

Our Vision

To be the leading and most trusted real estate and financial partner in Zambia, known for our commitment to client success, market expertise, and contribution to national development.
